# not right now

## Family

interaction

## Image cue

white square on red

## Prompt

please don't start talking to me.

## Notes

Red interaction badge. The wearer does not want others to initiate. They may still approach you, and you're welcome to respond when they do. Keep the square: the shape carries the meaning alongside the color, for colorblind readers. Meant to be shown, not kept face-down, and can be worn or flipped as live status. Lineage: color communication badges from Autism Network International and the first Autreat, Jim Sinclair, 1996 — https://stimpunks.org/access/interaction/
